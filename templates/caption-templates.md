## Variables
- {{hook_line}}: first strong line (0-3s)
- {{story_line}}: one sentence summary of story
- {{lesson_line}}: one sentence takeaway
- {{cta_line}}: explicit action prompt
- {{short_hook}}: 3-6 words
- {{seo_short_title}}: searchable short title
- {{topic_keyword}}: one keyword
